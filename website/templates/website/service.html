Our Workforce

1. To be a part of White Care Nursing staff

Our staff are carefully selected based on their dedication to these values, ensuring that each team member is passionate about their work, possesses a genuine interest in healthcare, and strives to make a positive impact on their communities. 2. Staff Recruitment

We hold the belief that everyone we work with and care for deserves the utmost excellence, and we take pride in sourcing the very best individuals for the job. To uphold our reputation, we have implemented rigorous recruitment and pre-screening procedures. These processes ensure that our staff possess the ideal blend of experience, qualifications, and attitude to meet both our high standards and the specific needs of our clients.

Employment background checks:
  • Face-to-face or online interview
  • Minimum six months of post qualification paid experience
  • Employment checks
  • 100 Point ID check
  • Minimum Two Professional Reference check
  • National Police check
  • Working with Children check
  • Working with Vulnerable People check
  • Work rights check
  • AHPRA Registration check for Nurses and Midwives
  • Completed Statutory Declaration
  • Immunisation
  • Annual flu vaccination certificate
  • Covid Vaccination
  • NDIS Screening Check
